What is PPC Advertising?

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is a powerful online marketing model where advertisers pay a fee each time one of their ads is clicked. It's an effective way to buy visits to your site, rather than attempting to 'earn' those visits organically. At Pabitra Technology, we specialize in creating and managing high-performing campaigns across platforms like Google Ads, Bing Ads, and social media channels to drive qualified leads and sales for your Bhubaneswar business.
